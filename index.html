ðŸ’¡ Fonts

  1. Reflect Brand Personality: Select fonts that align with your brand personality and convey the desired tone. For a professional and modern aesthetic, sans-serif fonts like "Roboto" offer clarity and a sleek appearance, while serif fonts like "Merriweather" can impart a classic and sophisticated feel.
  2. Prioritize Readability: Opt for fonts that prioritize readability across various devices and screen sizes. Sans-serif fonts are often preferred for body text due to their clean lines, while elegant serif fonts can be effective for headings to add a touch of sophistication.
  3. Enhance Visual Hierarchy: Establish a visual hierarchy by combining fonts with distinct styles and weights. Pairing a bold font for headings, such as "Montserrat Bold," with a readable body font like "Open Sans" creates a balanced and visually appealing design, guiding users through your content seamlessly.

ðŸ’¡ Text Styles

  1. h1 Title: Opt for the bold and modern "Lato" font with a weight of 900 for impactful and attention-grabbing titles. The deep blue color (#00334e) enhances visibility and conveys a sense of professionalism.
  2. h2 Subtitle: Utilize the sleek "Open Sans" font with a weight of 700 for subtitles, achieving a perfect balance between readability and style. The subtle blue hue (#004d73) adds a touch of sophistication and ensures a cohesive design.
  3. p Paragraph: Enhance readability in paragraphs with the versatile "Montserrat" font at a weight of 400, providing a clean and legible text. The classic black color (#000) maintains a timeless appearance, ensuring a comfortable reading experience for users.
